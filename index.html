My Broadcast Fan Page

Broadcast is the band name of an English musical group from Birmingham, consisting of Trish Keenan, James Cargill, Roj Stevens and Tim Felton, along with various drummers.

The Sound

Their music can be described as lo-fi psychedelic electronic, inspired by 1960's movie soundtracks and popular with people in the Mod and retro subcultures. Trish Keenan provides dreamy, hypnotic vocals. Although various synthesizers are used in this music, the drum tracks are live.

How I Found Them

Broadcast entered my world in 2003 through my college friend Mindi while attending art school together. Since then their music has been the accompaniment to many lovely drives, breezy beach days, and long bicycle rides.

A Sad Reminder

2011 came along and it was a very sad year for fans of Broadcast. Trish passed away from a sudden illness contracted while touring in Australia. Since her death James Cargill still carries the band name and has produced a soundtrack record (with no vocals) called Berberian Sound Studio.
