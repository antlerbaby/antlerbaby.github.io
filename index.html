Buttered Field Peas

Both field peas and black eyed peas can be prepared this way, as well as lima beans. Use fresh and/or frozen peas.

Required Supplies:

2 Cups Fresh or Frozen Field Peas, Rinsed and Thawed
1/2 White or Vidalia Onion, Cut into Squares
2 TBSP Beef Fat
1 TBSP Unsalted Butter
1/4 TSP Baking Soda
1 TSP High Quality Salt (Himalayan Pink, Celtic Grey, etc.)
1 Heavy Bottomed & Lidded Pot (Le Creuset-type)
1 Wooden Stirring Spoon (Without Sharp Edges)

Directions

  1. Fry the onions in 2 tablespoons of beef fat on medium-high heat, stirring constantly until slightly tan in color.
  2. Add peas and fry at medium heat for 10-15 minutes, stirring gently every 2-3 minutes to ensure the peas are exposed to the bottom of the pot. Keep lid on the pot in between stirring to retain moisture.
  3. Add the tablespoon of butter and stir gently until it is melted.
  4. Stir in 1/4 cup of water, 1 teaspoon of salt and 1/4 teaspoon of baking soda. Put on the lid and allow to simmer for 30-40 minutes stirring ocassionally.
  5. If peas are sticking to the bottom of the pot, reduce heat and/or add additional butter.
  6. Taste to add additonal salt or simmer longer if needed.
